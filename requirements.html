--- title: Requirements description: This is the list of requirements for the NEMO hrtf dataset that we have identified internally. ---

Usability

Permissevely licensed:

  Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

In various formats:

  SOFA, .mat, binary blob, .wav, etc.

In a centralised repository

  Github.

Flexibility

Model-based

  to guarantee extensibility, e.g. adding head-above-torso rotations.

Tunable

  e.g. adding a torso.

Adaptability

To guarantee post-adapatation good localization performance.

  i.e., the possibility of discriminating among directions should be maximal

What are we missing?

Help us complete the list of requirements to ease the use of the NEMO hrtf to all kinds of applications.

Send feedback.
